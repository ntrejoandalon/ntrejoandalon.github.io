Designing Rules for PowerPoints

Sep 6, 2023

Within this blogpost, we will be taking the slide below and making it more user friendly adapting the ideas for web design!

1. Remove distractions One major issue with the slide is the amount of colors, images, and differing sizes. There's a lot going on this slide and it's difficult to tell what is important. Let's remove some of that and see what it would look cleaned up. Here is the same slide with less distractions.

2. Remove unneccessary words That looks better but see the amount of text on that slide Who is going to read that. Excess text makes the slide harder to read as well as less appealing. A common rule for powerpoints is the seven seven seven rule. Seven words in seven lines readable at seven feet away. Another approach recommended in the book, remove half of the words. Here is the same slide with the text reduced.

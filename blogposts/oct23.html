VSCode and Accessibility

Oct 23, 2023
Directed Analysis

Within the reading, Krug states that accessible design is necessary as we can't expect none of our users to have an alternate requirement. Now, with that in mind, he states the best way to improve a site's accessibility for people that need alternate requirements is to improve it for everyone. In his own words, if someone has issues with a site and they don't need additional features, someone that does will still have difficulty following.

In this blogpost we're going to take some of the relevant suggestions Krug makes and apply them onto Visual Studio Code to see how accessible it is.

1. Make all content accessible by keyboard

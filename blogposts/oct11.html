Prototyping

Oct 11, 2023
Freeform

At this point, I've determined that Figma will be my defining skill by the end of the semester. Between the final project, design studio, and the redesign, I have spent a lot of hours creating components and adjusting design. I always thought that prototyping would be difficult, in IP I was in the Adobe camp, and that it would take up at least a week of time. Turns out, it is as simple as dragging the arrows across and setting an animation if necessary. It's a verb pleasent suprise about it and something I hope to use extensively when handling on click, on hover event. And while I have always seen figma as a static object, maybe with this all the designs can almost take a life of their own.
